Red Panda

Red Pandas are not Pandas...not even close.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 102.5 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: Temperate forests

They have long, soft, reddish-brown fur on the upper parts, blackish fur on the lower parts, and a light face with tear markings and robust cranio dental features. The light face has white badges similar to those of a raccoon, but each individual can have distinctive markings.
